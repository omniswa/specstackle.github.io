Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is your device data? +

We maintain a 99.9% data accuracy rate. All specifications are sourced from official manufacturers and verified through multiple channels. If you find an error, please report it to data@specstackle.top and we'll correct it immediately.

How often is the database updated? +

We update our database continuously as new devices are announced and released. New flagship devices are typically added within 24 hours of official announcement, while mid-range and budget devices are added within 1-2 weeks.

Can I use your data commercially? +

Our content is protected by copyright and is for personal, non-commercial use only. For commercial licensing or partnership inquiries, please contact partnerships@specstackle.top to discuss options.

How do you handle user privacy? +

Your privacy is our top priority. We don't sell or share your personal data. Your theme preference is stored locally on your device. For detailed information, please read our Privacy Policy.

Which brands do you cover? +

We currently track over many brands including Apple, Samsung, Google, OnePlus, Xiaomi, Motorola, Realme, OPPO, Vivo, Nothing, and many others. If you'd like us to add a specific brand, please reach out to us.
