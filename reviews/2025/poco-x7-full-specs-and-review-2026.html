POCO X7 Full Specs and Review (2026)

Published on March 25, 2026

The POCO X7 is a highly competitive mid-range smartphone that punches well above its weight class. Released in early 2025, it continues POCO’s legacy of delivering flagship-like features at an accessible price. Boasting a stunning 6.67-inch 1.5K curved AMOLED display protected by Gorilla Glass Victus 2, the X7 feels decidedly premium. Under the hood, it is powered by the capable MediaTek Dimensity 7300-Ultra chipset, ensuring smooth performance for both daily tasks and casual gaming. With a solid 50MP main camera equipped with OIS, an IP68 dust and water resistance rating, and a massive 5,110mAh battery supported by 45W fast charging, the POCO X7 provides an exceptional all-around experience for budget-conscious tech enthusiasts.

POCO X7 Pros & Cons

Pros:

  • Gorgeous 1.5K 120Hz curved AMOLED display with excellent peak brightness and Gorilla Glass Victus 2 protection.
  • An IP68 dust and water resistance rating is a rare and welcome addition in this price segment.
  • Excellent all-day battery life with a 5,110mAh capacity.
  • Reliable primary 50MP camera with OIS for sharp, stable photos.
  • Generous long-term software support, with promised updates stretching until 2029.

Cons:

  • The 2MP macro camera is lackluster and adds little value to the camera system.
  • It lacks expandable storage and a 3.5mm headphone jack.
  • The plastic frame and glossy finish on certain colorways can be major fingerprint magnets.
  • The included 45W charging speed is slightly slower compared to previous POCO generations.

Buy or Skip?

Who is the POCO X7 for? The POCO X7 is perfect for budget-conscious users, students, and media consumers who want a premium-looking phone without the flagship price tag. Its beautiful 1.5K AMOLED display and robust dual stereo speakers make it an excellent device for watching movies and scrolling through social media. Furthermore, its reliable MediaTek Dimensity 7300-Ultra processor handles daily multitasking and moderate gaming with absolute ease.

Who is it not for? If you are a hardcore mobile gamer aiming to play the most graphics-intensive games at maximum settings, you should skip this and look at the POCO X7 Pro or the POCO F-series instead. Additionally, mobile photography enthusiasts might find the secondary lenses (8MP ultrawide and 2MP macro) quite underwhelming, despite the solid performance of the 50MP main shooter.

Competitors and Alternatives

  • Redmi Note 14 Pro 5G: This is virtually the POCO X7's twin, sharing almost identical specifications. The choice between the two usually boils down to brand preference, design materials (POCO features its signature dual-tone styling), and regional availability.
  • Nothing Phone (2a): If you value a clean, bloat-free software experience and a unique transparent design with Glyph lighting, the Phone (2a) is a strong alternative. However, the POCO X7 outshines it with a higher resolution display and a superior IP68 rating.
  • Samsung Galaxy A35 5G: For those who prefer Samsung’s One UI ecosystem and slightly better camera software processing, the A35 is a great choice. Nevertheless, the POCO X7 offers faster charging, a more modern curved display, and generally better value for raw performance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does the POCO X7 support 5G?

Yes, the POCO X7 is a fully 5G-capable smartphone. It supports multiple 5G bands to ensure fast cellular internet connectivity, provided you are in an area with active 5G network coverage.

Is the POCO X7 waterproof?

The POCO X7 features an impressive IP68 rating for dust and water resistance. This means it can withstand being submerged in up to 1.5 meters of fresh water for up to 30 minutes, making it highly durable against accidental splashes or rain.

Does the POCO X7 have a headphone jack?

No, the POCO X7 does not come with a 3.5mm headphone jack. Users will need to rely on Bluetooth headphones or use a USB Type-C to 3.5mm adapter for wired audio listening.

Is the POCO X7 good for gaming?

The POCO X7 performs admirably for casual to moderate gaming thanks to its MediaTek Dimensity 7300-Ultra processor and vapor chamber cooling. While it can run popular titles like PUBG Mobile and Genshin Impact smoothly on medium settings, hardcore gamers might prefer the more powerful POCO X7 Pro.

Does the POCO X7 have expandable storage?

Unfortunately, the POCO X7 does not support microSD card expansion. However, it comes in generous base storage options of either 256GB or 512GB, which should be plenty of space for most users' apps, photos, and videos.
