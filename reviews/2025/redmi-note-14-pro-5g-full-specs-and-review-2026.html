Redmi Note 14 Pro 5G Full Specs and Review (2026)

Published on March 24, 2026

The Redmi Note 14 Pro 5G redefines the mid-range smartphone segment by borrowing key features usually reserved for flagship devices. Highlighted by its stunning 3D curved 1.5K AMOLED display and an incredibly durable build featuring Corning Gorilla Glass Victus 2 and an IP68 dust/water resistance rating, it is built to survive daily wear and tear. Powered by the MediaTek Dimensity 7300 Ultra and backed by a large battery, it provides an excellent balance of power efficiency and reliable daily performance.

Redmi Note 14 Pro 5G Specifications

Price Starting around $300 USD (₱17,999 in the Philippines)

Pros & Cons

Pros:

  • Premium build quality with an IP68 rating and Gorilla Glass Victus 2.
  • Excellent, vibrant 120Hz curved AMOLED display with minimal bezels.
  • Competent, battery-efficient MediaTek Dimensity 7300 Ultra processor.
  • Solid primary camera performance heavily stabilized by OIS.
  • Generous battery capacity easily ensures all-day usage.

Cons:

  • Lacks a microSD card slot for expandable storage.
  • No 3.5mm headphone jack.
  • Uses older UFS 2.2 storage technology, resulting in slower read/write speeds compared to modern flagships.
  • Regional specification variations (differing main cameras and battery capacities) can be confusing for buyers.

Buy or Skip?

Who is the Redmi Note 14 Pro 5G for?
This device is an excellent choice for media consumers, casual photographers, and everyday users who prioritize build quality and display. If you have a habit of dropping your phone or exposing it to water, the Gorilla Glass Victus 2 and IP68 rating offer tremendous peace of mind. It is also great for content consumption thanks to the high-brightness AMOLED screen and Dolby Atmos stereo speakers.

Who is it not for?
Hardcore mobile gamers should look elsewhere. While the Dimensity 7300 Ultra is reliable, it isn’t designed for pushing max frame rates on graphically demanding titles like Genshin Impact. Furthermore, audiophiles and users who like to hoard heavy local files might feel restricted by the lack of a 3.5mm headphone jack and expandable storage.

Competitors and Alternatives

  • POCO X6 Pro 5G / POCO X7 Pro: The sibling rival. POCO phones typically feature significantly faster processors (like the Dimensity 8300 Ultra) and faster storage technologies, making them much better for heavy gaming. However, the Redmi Note 14 Pro 5G easily wins in terms of design, camera stability, and durability (IP68 vs IP54).
  • Samsung Galaxy A55 5G: Samsung’s mid-range champion offers a cleaner software interface (One UI) and longer guaranteed software updates. However, the Galaxy A55 charges much slower at 25W, has noticeably thicker display bezels, and is often more expensive.
  • Realme 13 Pro 5G: A direct competitor that focuses heavily on camera design and AI photography features. While it stands toe-to-toe with the Redmi in terms of display quality, the Redmi Note 14 Pro 5G edges it out slightly when it comes to sheer ruggedness and water protection.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does the Redmi Note 14 Pro 5G have a headphone jack?

No, the Redmi Note 14 Pro 5G does not include a 3.5mm audio jack. Users will need to use a USB-C to 3.5mm adapter or rely on wireless Bluetooth headphones for audio playback.

Is the Redmi Note 14 Pro 5G waterproof?

Yes, the smartphone features an official IP68 rating for dust and water resistance. This means it can withstand being submerged in shallow, fresh water for a limited period, easily protecting it from rain, splashes, and accidental drops in water.

What processor is used in the Redmi Note 14 Pro 5G?

The device is powered by the MediaTek Dimensity 7300 Ultra. Built on a highly efficient 4nm manufacturing process, this octa-core chipset is designed to smoothly handle everyday multitasking, social media browsing, and moderate gaming without excessive battery drain.

Does the Redmi Note 14 Pro 5G support wireless charging?

Not specified. However, official spec sheets emphasize its fast 45W wired turbo charging capabilities to top up the battery quickly.

How long does the Redmi Note 14 Pro 5G battery last?

Thanks to its highly efficient processor and large 5,110 mAh (or 5,500 mAh in some regions) battery, the phone easily lasts a full day under heavy usage. With moderate use, many users can stretch the battery life to a day and a half before needing the included charger.
