HONOR X8d Full Specs and Review (2026)

Published on March 21, 2026

The HONOR X8d enters the 2026 midrange smartphone market with a clear focus on style, endurance, and practical durability. By combining a massive 7,000mAh battery with an incredibly slim 7.5mm profile, this device aims to provide days of use without the usual heft of a battery-focused phone. It features a stunning 6.77-inch 120Hz AMOLED display peaking at 3,000 nits, IP65 water and dust resistance, and SGS-certified drop protection up to 2 meters. While it lacks 5G connectivity, its Snapdragon 6s 4G Gen 2 processor, extensive AI tools baked into MagicOS 10, and reliable 108MP main camera make it an excellent choice for students, heavy media consumers, and outdoor workers.

HONOR X8d Pros & Cons

Pros:

  • Exceptional Battery Life: The 7,000mAh battery provides up to 52 hours of regular usage and 31 hours of continuous video playback.
  • Ultra-Slim Design: Despite the enormous battery capacity, the phone maintains a sleek 7.5mm profile and weighs only 188 grams.
  • Vibrant Display: The 120Hz AMOLED panel is bright and packed with eye-care technologies to prevent eye strain.
  • Robust Durability: The combination of an IP65 rating and a 2-meter drop resistance makes it a rugged daily driver.
  • AI Integration: A dedicated physical AI button offers quick access to a suite of intelligent tools like Circle to Search, AI Eraser, and AI Clean Up.

Cons:

  • No 5G Connectivity: The device relies solely on 4G LTE, which falls behind the 5G standard common in 2026 midrange phones.
  • Video Limitations: Video recording maxes out at 1080p without 4K capabilities.
  • No Expansion: Omits a microSD card slot and a 3.5mm headphone jack.
  • Average Processor: The Snapdragon 6s 4G Gen 2 easily handles daily tasks but can struggle with high-end gaming.

Buy or Skip?

Who is the HONOR X8d for? This smartphone is highly recommended for students, delivery riders, and frequent travelers who prioritize battery endurance and durability. With a battery that easily lasts up to two days and an ultra-slim chassis, it is perfect for multimedia consumers who want a large, vibrant display without needing to carry a bulky power bank.

Who is it not for? You should skip this device if you are a hardcore mobile gamer, a tech enthusiast, or a heavy content creator. The lack of 5G connectivity makes it less future-proof, and the processor is not built to run graphic-intensive games smoothly at maximum settings. Furthermore, the absence of 4K video recording might deter those who heavily rely on their smartphones for videography.

Competitors and Alternatives

  • HONOR X9d 5G: If you have a slightly higher budget, stepping up to the HONOR X9d is a logical move. It offers superior processing power and 5G connectivity while retaining HONOR’s signature drop-resistant design.
  • POCO X8 Pro: Available in a similar price bracket, this is a strong alternative for users who prioritize raw gaming performance and 5G access. However, opting for the POCO means sacrificing the extreme 7,000mAh battery endurance found in the X8d.
  • Infinix Note Edge 5G: Priced closely to the X8d, this Infinix model provides an entry point into 5G networks alongside a highly capable chipset. It serves as a more performance-oriented alternative but lacks the sleek 7.5mm design and the extensive AI editing suite of the HONOR X8d.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does the HONOR X8d support 5G?

No, the HONOR X8d does not support 5G networks. It relies on the Qualcomm Snapdragon 6s 4G Gen 2 chipset, which limits cellular connectivity to standard 4G LTE.

Is the HONOR X8d waterproof?

The device features an IP65 rating, meaning it is highly resistant to dust and can withstand low-pressure water jets and splashes. However, it is not completely waterproof and should never be fully submerged in water.

Does the HONOR X8d have a headphone jack or SD card slot?

No, the HONOR X8d does not come with a 3.5mm headphone jack or a microSD card slot for expandable storage. Users will need to rely on wireless audio, USB-C adapters, and the built-in 256GB internal storage.

How long does the HONOR X8d battery last?

With its massive 7,000mAh battery, the device boasts up to 52 hours of general usage and can handle up to 31 hours of continuous video playback on a single charge. It also features 45W wired fast charging to top up the battery efficiently when depleted.

What are the AI features of the HONOR X8d?

The smartphone includes a dedicated physical AI button for instantaneous access to built-in productivity commands. Through MagicOS 10, users get advanced tools like Circle to Search, AI Eraser, AI Cutout, AI Translate, and Google Gemini Assistant out of the box.
