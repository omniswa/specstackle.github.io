HONOR X9d Full Specs and Review (2026)

Published on March 13, 2026

Released in late 2025 and widely available in early 2026, the HONOR X9d 5G represents a significant leap forward for mid-range durability and battery endurance. Designed with a bulletproof-inspired shock-absorption framework and an industry-leading IP69K water and dust resistance rating, the device is built to handle extreme environments. It houses an unprecedented 8300 mAh Silicon-Carbon battery tucked behind a vivid 6.79-inch AMOLED display that hits up to 6000 nits of peak brightness. Powered by the efficient Snapdragon 6 Gen 4 chipset and featuring a capable 108MP main camera, the HONOR X9d caters to heavy users who prioritize extended screen time and absolute reliability over flashy flagship gimmicks.

HONOR X9d Pros & Cons

Pros:

  • Industry-leading 8300 mAh battery offers unmatched multi-day endurance.
  • Exceptional physical durability with an IP69K rating and 2.5-meter drop resistance.
  • High-quality 120Hz AMOLED screen with an incredibly bright 6000-nit peak brightness.
  • Reliable and highly efficient mid-range performance powered by the 4nm Snapdragon 6 Gen 4.
  • Generous base storage starting at 256GB.

Cons:

  • The 5MP ultrawide camera lags behind standard competitor offerings.
  • Lacks wireless charging capabilities.
  • No expandable storage via microSD card.
  • Does not include a dedicated telephoto lens.

Buy or Skip?

Who is the HONOR X9d for? If you work outdoors, have a habit of dropping your devices, or simply hate being tethered to a wall charger, the HONOR X9d is an absolute buy. It is tailor-made for delivery riders, field workers, and heavy media consumers who need a phone that can easily last up to two days on a single charge while surviving unpredictable weather and physical drops.

Who is it not for? Skip this phone if you are a mobile photography enthusiast or a hardcore competitive gamer. While the 108MP main sensor takes crisp daily photos, the 5MP ultrawide is relatively weak and it lacks the versatility of a telephoto lens. Additionally, while the Snapdragon 6 Gen 4 is efficient, it isn’t designed to max out graphic settings and frame rates on the most demanding 3D games.

Competitors and Alternatives

If you are shopping in the $300 to $400 mid-range market in 2026, the HONOR X9d faces stiff competition:

  • realme 14 5G: Powered by the same Snapdragon 6 Gen 4 chip, the realme 14 5G shifts the focus slightly toward gaming with a dedicated vapor cooling system and GT Boost software. It packs a smaller 6000mAh battery compared to the X9d, but brings a solid 50MP Sony main camera sensor.
  • Redmi Note 15 Pro 5G: Xiaomi’s primary offering utilizes the MediaTek Dimensity 7400-Ultra processor and a 6580mAh battery. The Redmi excels in the camera department with a massive 200MP main shooter, while still offering excellent durability (IP68/IP69K). It stands out as a better alternative if high-resolution mobile photography is your top priority.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does the HONOR X9d support wireless charging?

No, the HONOR X9d does not support wireless charging. It relies entirely on its 66W wired HONOR SuperCharge technology to replenish its massive 8300 mAh battery quickly.

Is the HONOR X9d waterproof?

Yes, it features an industry-leading IP69K, IP68, and IP66 rating for extreme water and dust resistance. This means it can withstand full submersion in water as well as high-pressure, high-temperature water jets.

Does the HONOR X9d have a headphone jack?

No, the device does not come with a 3.5mm headphone jack. Users will need to use a USB Type-C adapter or rely on Bluetooth 5.2 for their wireless audio connections.

Is the HONOR X9d good for gaming?

The smartphone handles casual and moderately demanding games very well thanks to the efficient Snapdragon 6 Gen 4 processor and smooth 120Hz display. However, hardcore gamers playing graphic-intensive titles at max settings might want to look for a device with a flagship-tier chipset.

Does the HONOR X9d have an expandable memory slot?

No, the HONOR X9d does not feature a microSD card slot for expandable storage. Fortunately, the device comes with ample base storage options of 256GB or 512GB to comfortably hold all of your apps, photos, and media.
