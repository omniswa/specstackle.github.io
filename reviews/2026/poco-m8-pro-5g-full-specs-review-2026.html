POCO M8 Pro 5G Full Specs & Review (2026)

Published on March 12, 2026

The POCO M8 Pro 5G, officially launched in January 2026, redefines the mid-range smartphone landscape by delivering flagship-grade durability and extraordinary battery life. Powered by the highly efficient 4nm Qualcomm Snapdragon 7s Gen 4 chipset, this device comfortably handles everyday multitasking, heavy media consumption, and moderate gaming. Its standout feature is undoubtedly the massive 6500mAh battery paired with ultra-fast 100W HyperCharge technology, which ensures multi-day use without long tethering to a wall outlet. Complemented by a vibrant 6.83-inch CrystalRes AMOLED display with a 120Hz refresh rate and extreme IP68/IP69K water resistance, the POCO M8 Pro 5G offers immense value for users seeking performance and rugged reliability on a budget.

POCO M8 Pro 5G Pros & Cons

Pros:

  • Endurance and Speed: The 6500mAh capacity easily lasts up to two days, while the 100W fast charger minimizes downtime.
  • Flagship-Level Durability: Carries an official IP68/IP69K rating, making it highly resistant to deep water immersion and high-pressure jets.
  • Exceptional Display: The 1.5K AMOLED panel reaches up to 3200 nits, offering incredible outdoor visibility and smooth scrolling.
  • Solid Mid-Range Performance: The Snapdragon 7s Gen 4 chipset strikes a great balance between power and thermal efficiency.

Cons:

  • Basic Camera Setup: Features a dual-camera system that lacks a dedicated telephoto or macro lens.
  • No Expandable Storage: Omission of a microSD card slot limits storage flexibility.
  • No 3.5mm Headphone Jack: Wired audio enthusiasts will need to rely on adapters or switch to Bluetooth.

Buy or Skip?

Who it is for: The POCO M8 Pro 5G is for power users, daily commuters, and mobile gamers who prioritize battery longevity, rapid charging speeds, and a large, vivid screen. It is also a fantastic option for outdoor workers or clumsy individuals, thanks to its military-grade IP68/IP69K durability and Gorilla Glass Victus 2 front.

Who it is not for: Skip this device if you are an avid mobile photographer who requires versatile camera focal lengths, such as optical zoom. Additionally, if you prefer smaller, one-handed smartphones or heavily rely on a 3.5mm headphone jack, this device will not align with your needs.

Verdict: Buy. At under $400, it dominates the mid-range segment for battery endurance, charging convenience, and display quality.

Competitors and Alternatives

  • Redmi Note 15 Pro 5G: Xiaomi’s internal rival sits in a similar price bracket but focuses on different priorities. While the POCO offers a superior 100W charging speed, the Redmi Note 15 Pro 5G appeals to content creators with a massive 200MP main camera and 4K recording. Choose the POCO for charging convenience, and the Redmi for photography.
  • Samsung Galaxy A56 5G: Samsung’s 2026 mid-ranger is powered by the Exynos 1580 and features a slightly smaller 6.7-inch Super AMOLED screen. The Galaxy A56 wins out on software ecosystem and a more versatile triple-camera setup (50MP main, 12MP ultrawide, 5MP macro). However, the POCO M8 Pro 5G outpaces it in battery size (6500mAh vs 5000mAh) and charging speed (100W vs 45W).

Frequently Asked Questions

Does the POCO M8 Pro 5G have a headphone jack?

No, the POCO M8 Pro 5G does not include a 3.5mm headphone jack. Users will need to use a USB-C dongle or rely on wireless Bluetooth headphones.

Is the POCO M8 Pro 5G waterproof?

Yes, it is highly water-resistant. The device carries an IP68 and IP69K rating, meaning it can withstand submersion in fresh water up to 1.5 meters for 30 minutes and resist high-pressure, high-temperature water jets.

What processor does the POCO M8 Pro 5G use?

It is powered by the Qualcomm Snapdragon 7s Gen 4 chipset, built on a 4nm process. This octa-core processor is designed to provide smooth multitasking and efficient battery consumption for mid-range smartphones.

Does the POCO M8 Pro 5G support wireless charging?

No, the POCO M8 Pro 5G does not support wireless charging. However, it compensates with blazing-fast 100W wired HyperCharge and supports up to 22.5W reverse wired charging for powering up your accessories.

What is the price of the POCO M8 Pro 5G?

The official price for the POCO M8 Pro 5G starts at approximately $367 in the global market. Regional pricing varies, with the base model costing around ₹36,999 in India and ₱16,999 in the Philippines.
