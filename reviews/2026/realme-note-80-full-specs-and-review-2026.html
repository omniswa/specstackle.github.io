realme Note 80 Full Specs and Review (2026)

Published on March 21, 2026

Launched in March 2026, the realme Note 80 is an ultra-durable, entry-level smartphone engineered for longevity and resilience. It distinguishes itself in the highly competitive budget segment through its massive 6300mAh battery and ArmorShell™ Protection, which provides MIL-STD-810H military-grade shock resistance. Powered by the 8-core Unisoc T7250 processor and featuring a 6.74-inch 90Hz display, the Note 80 prioritizes practical daily performance, multi-day battery life, and physical toughness over premium multimedia features.

realme Note 80 Specifications

Release Date March 2026

realme Note 80 Pros & Cons

Pros:

  • Exceptional Battery Life: The 6300mAh cell easily provides up to two days of heavy usage.
  • Rugged Build: IP54 splash resistance and MIL-STD-810H certification make it highly resistant to accidental drops and harsh environments.
  • Smooth Navigation: The 90Hz refresh rate ensures smooth scrolling for daily social media and web browsing.
  • Value Additions: Retains the 3.5mm headphone jack and supports expandable storage.

Cons:

  • Slow Charging: 15W charging is noticeably slow when trying to top up a battery of this size.
  • Basic Cameras: The 8MP rear and 5MP front cameras are only suitable for casual, well-lit photography.
  • Entry-Level Performance: The Unisoc T7250 and eMMC 5.1 storage struggle with heavy multitasking and demanding 3D gaming.
  • No 5G or NFC: Limited to 4G LTE networks and lacks contactless payment capabilities.

Buy or Skip?

Who is the realme Note 80 for? Buy this device if you are a gig worker, delivery rider, or someone who spends long hours outdoors. The combination of military-grade drop resistance, a 6300mAh battery, and a highly affordable price tag makes it a reliable tool that won’t die on you in the middle of a shift. It is also an excellent option as a secondary backup phone or a durable first smartphone for kids.

Who is it NOT for? Skip this device if you are a mobile gamer, content creator, or a power user. The entry-level processor and basic 8MP camera will not meet the demands of heavy processing, graphics-intensive games, or high-quality social media content creation. Those who need fast charging speeds should also look elsewhere, as the 15W limit will require patience.

Competitors and Alternatives

  • Xiaomi Redmi A3 / A4: These devices sit in a similar price bracket and offer cleaner designs. However, they lack the extreme 6300mAh battery capacity and military-grade durability of the Note 80.
  • POCO C65: A solid alternative if you want slightly better gaming performance and higher resolution cameras. The trade-off is a standard 5000mAh battery and a less rugged chassis.
  • Samsung Galaxy A06: Samsung offers a better software update policy and brand familiarity. It is slightly more expensive and does not feature the same shock resistance, but it delivers a more refined camera processing experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does the realme Note 80 support 5G connectivity?

No, the realme Note 80 is strictly a 4G LTE smartphone. It relies on the Unisoc T7250 chipset, which does not have a 5G modem equipped.

Is the realme Note 80 waterproof?

The device is not fully waterproof, but it does carry an IP54 rating. This means it is protected against dust ingress and can survive light water splashes, spills, or rain, but it should not be submerged in water.

How fast does the realme Note 80 charge?

It supports 15W wired charging via its USB Type-C port. Because the battery capacity is incredibly large at 6300mAh, a full charge from 0 to 100% will take roughly two and a half to three hours.

Is the realme Note 80 good for gaming?

The phone is capable of running lightweight or casual 2D games smoothly. However, due to its entry-level Unisoc T7250 processor and 4GB of RAM, it will struggle to run heavy 3D titles like Genshin Impact or Call of Duty: Mobile at high settings.

What colors are available for the realme Note 80?

The smartphone is available globally in two distinct color options: Glacier Blue and Storm Black. Both feature a flat-frame design measuring 7.94mm in thickness.
