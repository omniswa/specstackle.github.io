Vivo V50 Pro Full Specs and Review (2026)

Published on March 16, 2026

The Vivo V50 Pro is a premium mid-range smartphone that successfully bridges the gap between flagship performance and an accessible price point. Anticipated for an early to mid-2026 release, it is engineered primarily for photography enthusiasts and power users. The device features a vibrant 6.78-inch AMOLED display with a remarkably smooth refresh rate, all powered by the formidable MediaTek Dimensity 9300+ chipset. Its standout feature is the highly capable ZEISS-co-engineered triple 50MP camera setup, which ensures studio-quality portraits and exceptional low-light capabilities. Paired with a massive 5500 mAh battery and ultra-fast charging, the Vivo V50 Pro is positioned as one of the most compelling “flagship killers” on the market.

Vivo V50 Pro Pros & Cons

Pros:

  • Elite Camera Performance: The ZEISS-tuned triple 50MP camera system captures breathtaking portraits and detailed low-light shots.
  • Flagship Processing: The MediaTek Dimensity 9300+ handles heavy multitasking and graphics-intensive gaming with ease.
  • Exceptional Battery Life: A 5500 mAh cell easily lasts a full day, complemented by rapid 80W+ wired charging speeds.
  • Durable Build: An IP69 rating means it offers top-tier protection against dust and high-pressure water.
  • Stunning Display: The 1.5K AMOLED screen with a 144Hz refresh rate offers incredibly smooth scrolling and vivid media consumption.

Cons:

  • No Expandable Storage: The omission of a MicroSD card slot means you are limited to the internal storage.
  • No Headphone Jack: Audio relies entirely on USB-C or Bluetooth.
  • Slower USB Speeds: The USB Type-C port is limited to 2.0 standards, making large file transfers to a PC somewhat sluggish.
  • Pre-installed Software: Funtouch OS 15, while fluid, may still come with regional bloatware out of the box.

Buy or Skip?

Who is the Vivo V50 Pro for? The Vivo V50 Pro is a fantastic buy for mobile photography enthusiasts who want flagship-level camera performance—especially for portraits—without paying $1,000+ for top-tier flagships. It is also an excellent option for gamers and heavy media consumers who will benefit from the fast processor, large battery, and brilliant 144Hz AMOLED display.

Who is it not for? Skip this device if you prefer compact, pocket-friendly smartphones, as the 6.78-inch footprint makes it a notably large handset. It is also not the ideal choice for professional videographers who need fast USB 3.0+ transfer speeds for moving heavy 4K files, or for users who strictly prefer a stock, bloat-free Android experience.

Competitors and Alternatives

If you are considering the Vivo V50 Pro, you might also want to look at these alternatives in the same category:

  • OnePlus 13R / 14R: A perennial favorite in the flagship-killer space. The OnePlus provides a cleaner software interface (OxygenOS) and comparable performance for gaming. However, the Vivo V50 Pro generally outclasses it in camera versatility and portrait photography thanks to its ZEISS tuning.
  • Samsung Galaxy S25 FE / S26 FE: Samsung’s fan edition offers a highly refined software experience (One UI), longer guaranteed software support, and wireless charging. The trade-off is that Samsung usually features slower wired charging and thicker display bezels compared to the Vivo V50 Pro.
  • Xiaomi 14T Pro: Xiaomi’s top-tier T-series phone matches the Vivo with its own Leica-tuned lenses and ultra-fast charging capabilities. Choosing between the two mostly comes down to software preference (HyperOS vs. Funtouch OS) and aesthetic tastes.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the release date of the Vivo V50 Pro?

The Vivo V50 Pro is expected to be officially announced and released globally between March and May of 2026. Availability may roll out in phases depending on your specific region.

Is the Vivo V50 Pro waterproof?

Yes, the Vivo V50 Pro features a robust IP69 rating. This means it is fully dust-tight and can withstand high-pressure, high-temperature water jets, making it highly resilient against liquid damage.

Does the Vivo V50 Pro support wireless charging?

Not specified. Based on the spec sheets and previous models in the V-series lineup, the device focuses on ultra-fast wired charging (up to 100W). Wireless charging is typically reserved for Vivo’s more expensive X-series flagships.

Is the Vivo V50 Pro good for gaming?

Absolutely. Equipped with the MediaTek Dimensity 9300+ chipset and up to 12GB of LPDDR5X RAM, the Vivo V50 Pro is highly capable of running demanding mobile games at high frame rates without aggressive throttling.

Does the Vivo V50 Pro have a headphone jack?

No, the Vivo V50 Pro does not feature a 3.5mm headphone jack. Users will need to use wireless Bluetooth headphones or a USB-C to 3.5mm audio adapter to listen to music.
