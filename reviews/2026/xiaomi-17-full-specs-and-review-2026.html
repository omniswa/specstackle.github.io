Xiaomi 17 Full Specs and Review (2026)

Published on March 14, 2026

The Xiaomi 17 is a premium, compact flagship smartphone released globally in early 2026. In a bold marketing move, Xiaomi skipped the “16” moniker entirely to go head-to-head with the iPhone 17 series. Designed for users who want uncompromised power in a pocket-friendly form factor, the device features a stunning 6.3-inch LTPO CrystalRes AMOLED display with ultra-thin 1.18mm bezels and an incredible peak brightness of 3500 nits.

Under the hood, the Xiaomi 17 is the first smartphone to debut the 3nm Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 chipset, bringing massive improvements in raw performance and energy efficiency. It also shines in the photography department, boasting a co-engineered Leica Summilux triple-camera system featuring 50MP sensors across the board. Backed by a high-density silicon-carbon battery and running on Android 16-based HyperOS 3, the Xiaomi 17 pushes the boundaries of what a compact Android flagship can achieve.

Xiaomi 17 Specifications

Feature Details
Display 6.3-inch LTPO AMOLED (CrystalRes OLED), 1-120Hz refresh rate, 1.5K resolution, 3500 nits peak brightness, 12-bit color, HDR10+, Dolby Vision.
Processor Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 (3nm).
RAM/Storage 12GB / 16GB LPDDR5X RAM | 256GB / 512GB / 1TB UFS 4.1 Storage.
OS Xiaomi HyperOS 3 (based on Android 16).
Camera System (Rear) Triple Setup: 50MP Leica Main (1/1.28-inch, OIS, f/1.67) + 50MP Leica Ultra-wide (14mm) + 50MP Leica Floating Telephoto (60mm, 2.6x optical zoom).
Camera System (Front) 50MP sensor with 4K/60fps video recording support.
Battery & Charging 6,330 mAh (Global) / 7,000 mAh (China). Supports 100W Wired fast charging, 50W Wireless charging, and 22.5W Reverse wireless charging.
Connectivity 5G, Wi-Fi 7, Bluetooth 6.0, NFC, USB Type-C.
Build & Dimensions Aluminum frame, Matte Glass back, IP68 & IP69 dust/water resistance. Dimensions: 151.1 x 71.8 x 8.06 mm. Weight: 191g.
Release Date September 25, 2025 (China) | February 28, 2026 (Global).
Price ~$999 USD (€999 / ₹89,999).
Other Notable Features In-display fingerprint sensor, Dolby Atmos stereo speakers, Xiaomi Shield Glass protection, LIPO display packaging technology.

Xiaomi 17 Pros & Cons

Pros:

  • Elite Performance: Powered by the cutting-edge Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5, ensuring flawless multitasking and high-end gaming.
  • Exceptional Battery Tech: The high-density silicon-carbon battery (up to 7,000 mAh in some regions) is an industry-first for a device of this compact size.
  • Leica Camera Prowess: The 50MP triple-lens setup delivers pro-level cinematic imaging and excellent dynamic range.
  • Ergonomic Design: At just 6.3 inches and 191g, it is incredibly comfortable to use one-handed without sacrificing flagship features.

Cons:

  • Battery Disparity: Global users receive a slightly smaller 6,330 mAh battery compared to the 7,000 mAh cell found in the Chinese variant.
  • No Rear Display: The innovative secondary rear screen is strictly reserved for the Pro and Pro Max models.
  • Premium Pricing: With a starting price hovering around $1,000, it sits at the higher end of the compact smartphone market.

Buy or Skip?

Buy it if: You are looking for a highly capable, compact flagship smartphone. The Xiaomi 17 is perfect for users who want top-tier processing power, exceptional all-day battery life, and high-end photography without dealing with the bulk of a 6.8+ inch device.

Skip it if: You are a power user who wants the absolute pinnacle of smartphone photography or a massive screen. If you prefer utilizing extreme optical zoom (like a 200MP periscope lens) or want the novelty of a rear secondary display, you should consider upgrading to the Xiaomi 17 Ultra or the Xiaomi 17 Pro Max instead.

Competitors and Alternatives

If you are shopping in this premium category, the Xiaomi 17 faces strong competition:

  • iPhone 17 Pro: Apple’s direct rival in the compact flagship space, offering a similar screen size, seamless iOS integration, and the A19 chip.
  • Samsung Galaxy S26: The primary Android alternative, offering a comparable compact form factor, Samsung’s robust Galaxy AI features, and a slightly different aesthetic approach.
  • Xiaomi 17 Ultra: For those willing to spend more and carry a larger 6.9-inch device, the Ultra variant offers an unparalleled 200MP Leica telephoto lens and an even more sophisticated camera array.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Xiaomi 17 water-resistant?

Yes, the Xiaomi 17 is highly durable and features both IP68 and IP69 ratings. This means it is fully protected against dust, accidental submersion, and even high-pressure, high-temperature water sprays.

Does the Xiaomi 17 have a rear secondary screen?

No, the dynamic rear secondary display is an exclusive feature found only on the Xiaomi 17 Pro and Xiaomi 17 Pro Max. The standard Xiaomi 17 opts for a clean, traditional matte glass back.

What is the battery capacity of the Xiaomi 17?

The global version of the Xiaomi 17 comes with a massive 6,330 mAh battery, which is incredibly large for a compact phone. However, the Chinese variant features a slightly larger 7,000 mAh silicon-carbon cell. Both versions support 100W ultra-fast wired charging.

Why did Xiaomi skip the 16 series?

Xiaomi intentionally skipped the “Xiaomi 16” naming convention to align its product numbering directly with Apple’s iPhone 17 series. This strategic move was made to position the device as a direct, equivalent competitor in the 2026 flagship market.

Does the Xiaomi 17 come with Google apps?

Yes, the global version of the Xiaomi 17 comes fully equipped with Google Mobile Services (GMS). It runs on Xiaomi’s HyperOS 3 out of the box, with full access to the Google Play Store and other essential Google applications.
