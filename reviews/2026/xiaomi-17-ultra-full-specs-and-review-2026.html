Xiaomi 17 Ultra Full Specs and Review (2026)

Published on March 14, 2026

The Xiaomi 17 Ultra stands as one of the most uncompromising photography-centric smartphones of 2026. Co-engineered with Leica, this flagship device is headlined by a 1-inch 50MP Light Fusion 1050L main sensor and a groundbreaking 200MP APO telephoto lens featuring continuous optical zoom. Beyond its towering camera prowess, the Xiaomi 17 Ultra offers exceptional everyday performance driven by the latest Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 chipset, a vibrant 6.9-inch 3500-nit OLED display, and a massive silicon-carbon battery. Housed in a refined, IP69-rated ultra-thin chassis, it is engineered for power users and mobile photography enthusiasts who demand the absolute best in hardware.

Xiaomi 17 Ultra Pros & Cons

Pros:

  • Industry-Leading Cameras: The combination of a 1-inch primary sensor with LOFIC technology and a 200MP continuous optical zoom telephoto lens produces unmatched detail and dynamic range.
  • Massive Battery: The inclusion of a 6000mAh (global) silicon-carbon battery ensures easily over a day of heavy use without adding bulk to the device.
  • Brilliant Display: The 6.9-inch OLED panel reaches an incredibly bright 3500 nits, making it effortless to use in direct sunlight.
  • Extreme Durability: Moving beyond standard flagship protection, the IP69 rating ensures it survives high-temperature, high-pressure water jets.

Cons:

  • Premium Price Tag: The ultra-premium pricing puts it out of reach for budget-conscious buyers.
  • Overwhelming Software: The native camera app is brimming with an overwhelming amount of Leica filters, settings, and pro-level modes that may confuse a casual user.
  • Bulky Camera Module: Due to the massive optical hardware, the rear circular camera deco adds notable top-heaviness to the phone.
  • Limited Availability: Official retail availability and carrier support in the US remain non-existent, requiring complicated importing.

Buy or Skip?

Who is the Xiaomi 17 Ultra for? This phone is explicitly designed for mobile photography purists and videographers. If you actively seek out superior camera hardware, shoot in manual modes, and value the consistency of continuous optical zoom, the Xiaomi 17 Ultra is an absolute buy. It is also a fantastic choice for power users who demand long battery life to complement extreme display brightness and top-tier processor speeds.

Who is it not for? Skip the Xiaomi 17 Ultra if you prefer lightweight, compact smartphones or a straightforward point-and-shoot camera experience. At 6.9 inches with a hefty camera bump, it is unwieldy for one-handed use. Additionally, buyers in the US should skip this device due to the lack of official warranties and potential 5G network band incompatibilities.

Competitors and Alternatives

  • Samsung Galaxy S26 Ultra: The default Android alternative. While it lacks the 1-inch main sensor and continuous optical zoom found on the Xiaomi, the S26 Ultra offers a higher-resolution display, integrated S Pen productivity, and wider global availability.
  • Apple iPhone 17 Pro Max: The logical choice for iOS loyalists. The iPhone 17 Pro Max dominates in raw video recording consistency, ecosystem integration, and app optimization, offering a more streamlined experience than Xiaomi’s HyperOS.
  • Vivo X300 Pro / Oppo Find X9 Pro: For those already importing Chinese flagships, these alternatives directly rival Xiaomi’s camera capabilities. They offer competitive imaging technologies and premium builds, often with slightly different color science and focal length priorities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does the Xiaomi 17 Ultra have a global version?

Yes, Xiaomi officially launched the global version of the 17 Ultra at the Mobile World Congress on February 28, 2026. The global variant is slightly adjusted, featuring a 6000mAh battery compared to the 6800mAh cell found in the China-exclusive model.

Is the Xiaomi 17 Ultra waterproof?

The Xiaomi 17 Ultra offers some of the highest durability ratings on the market with IP66, IP68, and IP69 certifications. This ensures it is protected not only against dust and submersion in water, but also against high-temperature and high-pressure water sprays.

What is the camera zoom capability of the Xiaomi 17 Ultra?

The device utilizes a first-of-its-kind 200MP Leica APO telephoto lens that features seamless continuous optical zoom. This allows you to mechanically zoom anywhere between 3.2x and 4.3x (75mm to 100mm) without losing optical quality, while also supporting an 8.6x high-quality extension.

Does the Xiaomi 17 Ultra support wireless charging?

Yes, the smartphone is equipped with 50W wireless HyperCharge, allowing for rapid cord-free top-ups. It also supports 90W fast wired charging and a 10W reverse wireless charging feature to power up accessories like earbuds.

Will the Xiaomi 17 Ultra be available in the US?

Official US availability for the Xiaomi 17 Ultra is not specified, as Xiaomi historically refrains from selling its smartphones directly in the United States. US consumers interested in the device will need to import the global version, keeping in mind the potential lack of warranty and limited network band support.
